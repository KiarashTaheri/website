About Me:

Hi and welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Jaiden and I am an Engineering Student at the University of British Columbia (UBC) who is specializing my studies in Biomedical, Electrical and Software Engineering. This site is intended to document my coursework and projects throughout my studies, to share useful resources, and to help people have a better sense of who I am.

Some of the things I'm fascinated with include (and not limited to) are: robotics, brain-computer interfaces (BCIs), business intelligence, and neurotechnology. I hope to pursue a profession in which I work with other people who strive to look beyond traditional and rigid structures to work across many boundaries in business, engineering and science. I strive to transcend those boundaries and hope to be able to form meaningful connections with others who strive to create and pioneer novel solutions that tackle real-world problems while forging the future of possibilities.
